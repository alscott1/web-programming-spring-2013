Beautiful Beaches

Long sandy beaches and rocky jagged cliffs are just a few things that make the rugged Sonoma Coast a wonderful place to visit. There are dozens of hiking trails up and down the coast as well as many beaches. If your looking for a place to relax, and enjoy nature's stunning beauty, the Sonoma Coast is the perfect place for you.

The Russian River

The river provides wildlife habitat including warm and cold freshwater habitat for fish migration and spawning.The Russian River is the largest river in the Central California Coast Steelhead trout Distinct population segment. Many people enjoy kayaking and swimming in this glorious northern California treasure!

Northern California Wine Tasting

Sonoma County is known for having some of the best wine in the country. The temperate (Ocean) climate produces great Pinot Noir and fantastic champagne.

Beautiful, Colorful, Sonoma County

Sonoma County, California is one of the most diverse counties that you will ever encounter. This spectacular location is made up of gorgeous beaches, towering redwoods, the beautiful Russian River, as well as dozens of famous northern California wineries.

Experience the Sonoma Coast and its neighboring cities first hand. Enjoy fine dining, exceptional wines, and of course, breath-taking scenery to go with it.
